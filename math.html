List of Left-Aligned Mathematical Functions

  • The variance ($\sigma^2$) is calculated as: $$ \sigma^2 = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=1}^{N} (x_i - \mu)^2 $$
  • The Gaussian integral is defined by: $$ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi} $$
  • The derivative definition uses a limit function: $$ f'(x) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x + \Delta x) - \sin(x)}{\Delta x} $$
